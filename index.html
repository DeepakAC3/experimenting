Our Heritage & Mission

GAJAHA SKYCART4U was born from a deep passion for preserving and sharing India's rich cultural heritage. Founded in Chennai, our journey began with a simple mission: to connect authentic Indian artisanal treasures with customers worldwide.

We work directly with skilled artisans across India, from the traditional doll makers of Tamil Nadu to the master weavers of Kanjeevaram. Every product in our collection tells a story of generations of craftsmanship, cultural significance, and artistic excellence.

Our commitment extends beyond commerce - we're dedicated to supporting local artisan communities, preserving traditional crafts, and ensuring that these beautiful art forms continue to thrive for future generations.
